💡 Heart Health Tips
💧 Stay Hydrated
Dehydration can raise your resting heart rate by 5–8 BPM. Drink 2–3L of water daily.
🧘 Manage Stress
High stress elevates cortisol which increases heart rate. Practice deep breathing or yoga daily.
🏋️ Exercise Regularly
Consistent aerobic exercise lowers your resting heart rate over time — a sign of improved fitness.

💡 Sleep Tips
📱
No Screens Before Bed
Blue light suppresses melatonin by up to 50%. Stop screens 1 hour before sleep.
🌡️
Keep Room Cool (18–20°C)
A cooler room signals your body it's time to sleep. Open windows or use a fan.
⏰
Consistent Schedule
Same sleep/wake time every day — even weekends — regulates your circadian rhythm.
☕
No Caffeine After 2 PM
Caffeine half-life is 5–6 hours. A 4 PM coffee still affects you at 10 PM.
